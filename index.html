Hans Von Gruenigen's 3D Brick Game

Controls:

  • MOUSE - Controls camera
  • W - Shift cubies in
  • S - Shift cubies out
  • A - Shift cubies left
  • D - Shift cubies right
  • Q - Shift cubies up
  • E - Shift cubies down
  • ENTER - Add a random cubie somewhere
  • SHIFT - Print debug info
* all controls are realative to the front of the cube(highlighted)
